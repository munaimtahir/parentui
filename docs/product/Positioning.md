# Positioning

## Primary positioning

> A safer home screen for your child’s first phone.

## Expanded positioning

EasyUI Guardian Launcher helps parents give children access to calls, learning apps, emergency contacts, camera, and approved apps — without exposing the full smartphone experience at once.

## Positioning rules

Use this language:

- child-safe launcher
- safer home screen
- guided independence
- parent-approved apps
- routine-based phone use
- calmer phone experience
- first phone setup
- focus-friendly home screen

Avoid this language:

- spy on your child
- monitor everything
- track your child
- block everything
- impossible to bypass
- full lockdown
- hidden surveillance
- enterprise kiosk
- total phone control

## Parent-facing promise

Give your child access to only what they need — calls, learning apps, camera, and approved apps — without the clutter of a full smartphone.

## Child-facing feeling

The app should feel safe, calm, useful, and understandable.

It should not feel like punishment.

## Competitor relationship

This app should not compete directly with Google Family Link at MVP stage.

Preferred framing:

> Use EasyUI Guardian Launcher for a simpler child home screen. Use Android settings or Google Family Link for deeper system-level restrictions.

## Store listing headline candidates

1. A safer home screen for your child’s first phone.
2. Give your child a simpler, parent-approved phone.
3. Calls, learning apps, emergency access, and approved apps in one calm launcher.
4. Help your child use a phone without opening the whole smartphone experience.
5. A simple launcher for guided child independence.

## One-sentence investor/team description

EasyUI Guardian Launcher is an offline-first Android launcher that helps parents set up a child-safe home screen with approved apps, emergency access, parent contacts, and routine-based modes.
