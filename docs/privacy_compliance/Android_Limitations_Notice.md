# Android Limitations Notice

## Purpose

This text should guide onboarding, help screens, Play Store wording, and parent-facing documentation.

## Plain English notice

EasyUI Guardian Launcher changes the home screen experience. It helps your child see only the apps and actions you choose inside this launcher.

It does not fully control every part of Android.

Depending on the device, Android version, and system settings, a child may still access some phone areas outside the launcher, such as notifications, system settings, or recently opened apps.

For deeper device-level restrictions, use Android system settings or Google Family Link together with this launcher.

## What the app can do

- replace the home screen
- show a simple child home screen
- show parent-approved apps
- hide apps from this launcher’s child view
- protect launcher settings with parent PIN
- provide parent and emergency shortcuts
- provide routine-based modes

## What the app cannot honestly promise

- block every Android setting
- block the notification shade on every device
- prevent all app launching outside the launcher
- prevent uninstall on every consumer device
- replace Google Family Link
- provide enterprise-grade kiosk lockdown
- guarantee that the phone cannot be bypassed

## Store wording rule

Never use:

- impossible to bypass
- full lockdown
- total control
- complete protection
- blocks everything
- child cannot access anything else

Use:

- simplifies the home screen
- limits what appears in the launcher
- helps parents create a safer phone surface
- works best with Android settings or Family Link for deeper controls
